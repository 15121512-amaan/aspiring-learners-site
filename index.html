About Us

Welcome to Aspiring Learners Coaching Institute, a trusted name in academic excellence and entrance exam preparation. Founded with a mission to empower students through quality education, we cater to Classes 6 to 12 across major subjects. We also offer dedicated coaching for JMI, AMU, and CUET entrance exams.

Our institute is led by Er. Mohd Amaan, an experienced educator and tech professional with over 6 years of teaching experience. Amaan Sir holds a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering, followed by an M.Tech with specialization in Cloud Computing. His background in both academics and technology allows him to bring a modern, result-oriented teaching approach into the classroom.

He is known for his clarity of concepts, motivational sessions, and deep commitment to student success.
